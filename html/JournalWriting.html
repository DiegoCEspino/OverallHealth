Three Good Things (TGT) or What-Went-Well is a gratitude journal exercise that cultivates gratitude, increases optimism, and boosts happiness. Method: Before going to bed at night, sit for a while and look back on your day. Think and write 3 things that went well. Reflect on them. Repeat for at least a week.
